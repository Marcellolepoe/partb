? 2. Immediate Disclosure – LR 703–704
?? When to Refer: “Does this event require immediate announcement?” “Can it be withheld?”
?? LR 703(1): General Disclosure Obligation
Must disclose immediately if:
* (a) Needed to avoid a false market
* (b) Likely to materially affect price/value of securities
Applies to:
* Issuer, subsidiaries (>50%), associated companies (20–50%)

?? LR 703(4): Examples of Material Info
* Financial condition
* M&A, JV, or litigation
* Change in CEO/major shareholder
* Loss of key customer
* Anything affecting shareholder rights
?? Common in exams: Manager resignation, material contract loss, litigation

?? LR 704: Mandatory Events to Disclose
* Appointments/resignations (director, CEO, CFO, auditors)
* Amendments to constitution
* Shareholder meetings
* Auditor qualifications
?? Red Herring Trap: Media release ? proper disclosure. Must use SGXNET (LR 702).

?? LR 703(2)-(3): Exceptions to Disclosure
ExceptionExplanationLR 703(2)Prohibited by law – e.g. court gag orderLR 703(3) – 3-part test (ALL must be satisfied):1) A reasonable person would not expect disclosure
2) Info is confidential
3) Info is incomplete, speculative, internal, or trade secret?? Frequently Tested: Must disclose once any of the 3 conditions no longer hold.

? 4. General Disclosure Practices
?? When to Refer: Trading halt? Rumour clarification? Analyst briefing?
?? Trading Halts
* Min: 30 mins | Max: 3 market days
* Used to facilitate disclosure of material info

?? Rumours / Market Activity
* Must clarify if unusual trading occurs due to leaks

?? Selective Disclosure
* Prohibited to leak material info to analysts/media
* Even if info is “already known”, must still publish on SGXNET

? 6. IPT Exceptions – LR 915–916
?? When to Refer: Lease? Utility purchase? Public tender?
?? LR 915 – Full Exemptions
No announcement, approval or AR disclosure required:
* Dividends, splits, rights issues
* Employee stock options
* Utility purchases (fixed public pricing)
* Loans from licensed banks on arm’s length terms

? 10. Mandatory Director Training – Practice Note 2.3
?? When to Refer: “Does this director need training?”
?? Required if:
* First-time director of listed company
* Complete within 1 year of appointment
?? Exemption:
* NC assesses equivalent experience
* Must disclose reasons in AR / announcement
?? SGX can still require training despite exemption

? 11. Remuneration Disclosure – Code 8.1, LR 1207(10D)
?? When to Refer: “What level of detail must be disclosed?”
?? Code Provision 8.1 – Comply or Explain
* Disclose CEO + each director: name + amount + breakdown
* Top 5 key management (non-directors): in bands + aggregate total

?? LR 1207(10D) – Mandatory (From FY ending ? 31 Dec 2024)
* Names, amounts, % breakdown (base, bonus, benefits, shares)
* Covers directors + CEO + subsidiary pay too
?? Frequently Tested: Band disclosure ? sufficient post-2024
